Services

Personal pages

I offer the development of a personal or business presentation page — a simple, fast, and cost-effective solution for professionals or companies looking to showcase their services online. The page you’re currently viewing is a perfect example of this service. The site is built as a single, responsive static page tailored to your needs, and includes basic customization such as your logo, contact info, and service descriptions. This service is ideal for those who need a clean and professional online presence without the complexity of a full website.

Static websites

I offer the development of multi-page static websites tailored for businesses or individuals who need a fast, lightweight, and reliable online presence. These websites can include blogs, informational pages, service descriptions, or any other static content. They are ideal for projects that don’t require complex backend functionality but still demand a clean, professional look and responsive design. Each site is custom-built to match your branding and structure, ensuring an optimal user experience and fast loading times.

E-commerce development

I offer eCommerce development services using either Shopify or custom-built solutions, depending on your business needs. Shopify is a great choice for those who want to launch quickly, as the platform provides a solid foundation with built-in features that speed up the development process. For more tailored requirements, I also build eCommerce sites from scratch, offering full flexibility and custom functionality. Whether you're starting a new online store or upgrading an existing one, I can help you create a professional, scalable, and user-friendly shopping experience.

Custom web apps

I offer custom web applications development, either public or private, designed to meet the specific needs of your business or project. These applications can include features such as user registration and login, admin dashboards, database management, and more. Whether you need a business management system or a website with secure user access, I build scalable, efficient solutions hosted on cloud platforms like AWS. Each application is developed with performance, security, and usability in mind to ensure long-term reliability and easy maintenance.
